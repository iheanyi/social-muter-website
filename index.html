Take control of your Twitter Timeline.

Tired of Twitter spoiling that new episode of your favorite TV show every week? Or, are you tired of seeing people talk about somebody or something (*cough* Donald Trump)? Social Muter is here for you. We'll clean up your Twitter feed based on keywords or phrases that you specify.
